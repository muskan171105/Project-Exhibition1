Heart

Functioning of Heart

The heart is a vital organ that pumps blood throughout the body. It has four chambers that work together to move blood. The right side pumps blood to the lungs to get oxygen, while the left side pumps oxygen-rich blood to the body. This constant circulation ensures that every cell receives
the oxygen and nutrients needed for survival. The heart also helps remove carbon dioxide and other waste products through the blood. Its rhythmic contractions are regulated by electrical impulses, keeping it beating tirelessly day and night. Read more

Problems of Heart

Heart problems occur when the heart cannot work properly. Common issues include blocked blood vessels, irregular heartbeats, or weak heart muscles. These problems can lead to chest pain, shortness of breath, or tiredness. High blood pressure, smoking, and unhealthy diets can increase the risk of heart issues. Read more

Precautions for Heart

Taking care of your heart is essential for a healthy life. Maintain a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins to support cardiovascular health. Regular exercise, such as walking, jogging, or yoga, can strengthen your heart and improve circulation. Avoid smoking and limit alcohol consumption, as they can damage blood vessels and increase heart disease risk. Manage stress through relaxation techniques, mindfulness, or hobbies to keep your blood pressure in check. Read more
